What’s Art and Wine?

Discover the magic of Los Cabos with an art and wine session led by a local artist. Sip on delicious local wines as you paint with guidance from the artist. Enjoy the beautiful views and chat with fellow participants while creating your masterpiece. Leave with your artwork and wonderful memories of your time in Los Cabos.

What’s included in each session?

  • 2 hours of guided class by an invited artist.
  • 2 glasses of wine, red or white wine.
  • One canvas 30 x 30 cm.
  • Music and a chill atmosphere!
